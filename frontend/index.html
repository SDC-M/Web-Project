Welcome on Kuva

The social network dedicated to sharing and annotating photos

Kuva Features

Share Photos

Easily share your photos with your friends or community. Publish your favorite photos, whether they are from your travels, events, or daily moments.

Annotate your Photos

Add text annotations to your photos. Comment, tell a story, or share additional information to enrich the visual experience.

Us

We are Matéo and Sébastien, two undergraduate computer science students in our second year at university. Kuva is our web development project, created as part of our coursework. It reflects both our passion for technology and our interest in creating meaningful user experiences. Our goal was to build a simple, intuitive, and engaging platform where people can share and annotate their favorite photos.

Through Kuva, we've not only developed our technical skills in web development but also learned the importance of user interface design, teamwork, and creative thinking. We hope you enjoy using Kuva as much as we enjoyed building it!
